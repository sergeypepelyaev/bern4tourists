Activities

Urban Beavers & Breakfast Tour

Start your morning with a guided walk along the Aare River, spotting Bern's elusive urban beaver colonies. Local ecologists share quirky facts while you sip fresh elderflower tea. The tour ends with a riverside breakfast of Zopf, alpine cheese, and wild berry jam. It's a serene, nature-meets-city experience that feels like a secret locals-only ritual.

Clocktower Acoustics Experience

Inside Bern's medieval Zytglogge, a sound engineer hosts twilight sessions where you explore the acoustics of the ancient clockwork. You'll hear how different musical instruments resonate through the stone chamber—from alpine horns to glass harmonicas. The finale is a short ambient concert synced with the hourly chime. It's part science, part magic.

Fondue & Folklore Under the Bridge

Beneath the Nydegg Bridge, storytellers gather at dusk for “Fondue & Folklore,” a candlelit evening of Swiss myths and melted cheese. You'll hear tales of Bern's bear guardians and ghostly monks while dipping bread into bubbling cauldrons. Local musicians add eerie alpine tunes. It's cozy, weird, and unforgettable.

Einstein's Basement Lab Reenactment

In a tucked-away cellar near Kramgasse, actors recreate Einstein's early Bern years with a hands-on “lab” experience. You'll scribble fake patent office notes, play with vintage physics puzzles, and even simulate a light-speed experiment using mirrors and lasers. It's nerdy fun with just enough historical flavor to feel legit.
